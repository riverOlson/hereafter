record their life

Forgetting someone over time is one of the hardest parts of loss. Collecting as many memories of them in one place as soon as possible will not fully stop this process, but it will provide a lot of comfort in the future. Providing as much information as possible will ensure that their legacy persists beyond death as their mark on the world is solidified.

organize your memories

Each memory is associated with the approximate date it happened, allowing you to collect their life's story easily even if you don't remember it in order on your own. Dates can also be changed after a memory has been recorded in case you misremembered the first time as well. After all, mistakes are a normal part of life.

leave their truth behind

Since these memory journals are incredibly important to our customers, we give them as much input as possible. Additionally, we allow multiple contributors for each journal to get as many people as possible to pool their memories. We want to make sure that we are accurately representing them as they lived.

our promise

ethics and integrity

The dead deserve nothing but our utmost love and care. None of your data will be reviewed or used by our team in any way unless you ask us to do so to restore data. We will never sell it to external buyers either, since we make enough revenue to sustain ourselves without going behind our customers' backs.

get help with grief

select your experiences

Every grieving process is different, but there does tend to be common experiences and issues that we may be able to help you with. The stages of grief, though not fully accurate to the complicated and nonlinear process of dealing with grief, may allow us to give you that help.

Clicking on the respective buttons for each stage will show you relevant advice if you are primarily dealing with any given stage. Since these stages rarely ever happen in order and often overlap, we have prepared for any combination of two of these stages to be combined.

Feel free to come back and consult this page again later if your situation happens to change.
